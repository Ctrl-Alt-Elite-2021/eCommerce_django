Visit Us

We couldn't be prouder to open the doors of our very first store, located at 10 Prince Street just a few blocks from the SoHo office where our team dreams up all of our pieces. Designed in collaboration with our friend, Poonam, this space is full of our favorite design elements, from a beautiful custom seating area perfect for trying on our footwear to the pleated tissue paper used during the pleating process in our own signature heels. If you're in the area, we hope you stop by for a visit and find something you love.

On Safety & Accessibility

Our store is ADA compliant and accessible by wheelchair. If you need assistance entering the shop, please contact our team and we will do whatever we can to accommodate your visit.

Please note, to keep our team and customers safe, our capacity is limited and we require masks within the store at all times. If you're feeling under the weather, please reschedule and we'd be happy to see you in the future!

Exclusive To Our Store

We're excited to offer exclusive styles and early availability on select pieces in our store. Ready-to-Wear is available to shop in-person for the first time in the boutique, as well as a hand-picked selection of third party good from Jessie's favorite small businesses. We also offer same day messenger service for store inventory within New York City for a delivery fee, making it even easier to stock up on these special offerings.

Click below to reserve an appointment with one of our stylists in person at 10 Prince or virtually via Zoom. Please be sure to specify in the notes any items you are particularly interested in so we can be prepared for your appointment.
